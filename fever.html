Fever -Medications

Fever, also referred to as pyrexia, is defined as having a temperature above the normal range due to an increase in the body's temperature set point.

An abnormally high body temperature, usually accompanied by shivering, headache, and in severe instances, delirium. "she had a slight fever"

Symptoms

  • Sweating.
  • Chills and shivering.
  • Headache
  • Muscle aches.
  • Loss of appetite.

Medications

  • DOLO -650
  • Paracetamol
  • acetaminophen
  • Tylenol
  • aspirin
